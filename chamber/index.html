Claresholm Chamber of Commerce

Site Name

The site name selected is "Claresholm Chamber of Commerce." This name is chosen to clearly represent the locale, Claresholm, and to highlight the purpose of the site, which is to support and promote local businesses and community growth.

Site Purpose

The Claresholm Chamber of Commerce website will serve two main purposes:

  • To act as a hub for local businesses, providing resources, networking opportunities, and information on community events.
  • To promote economic and community development within Claresholm by offering support and services to local businesses and fostering community engagement.

Scenarios

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What are the recent developments and projects undertaken by the chamber to enhance local businesses?

Color Schema

The color schema for the website will be as follows:

  • Moth Green - used for headers, footers, and background accents.
  • Blue - used for primary buttons, links, and important callouts.
  • Yellow - used for highlights and secondary buttons.
  • Light Gray - used for background color and text areas.

Typography

The typography choices for the website will be:

  • Montserrat - used for headings and important titles.
  • Open Sans - used for body text and general content.
